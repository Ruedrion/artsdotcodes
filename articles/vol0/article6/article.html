BabyCastles

Babycastles is a collective with roots in New York's D.I.Y. culture

We're excited to talk with Syed Salahuddin about the renowned DIY hacker, gamer, art spaced baby castles about their almost recent new digs in Manhattan and their current success becojming a a registered 501C3 non-profi. Babycastles is a collective with roots in New York's D.I.Y. culture dedicated to building platforms for diversity in video games culture at every level from creators to consumers, connecting the independent game developer community with the broader New York art community, identifying exciting new voices in game creation from around the world and providing them exposure to new audiences. Stay tuned for an insightful and whimsical interview with one of the founders.
